See who's not following you back on instagram

First you need to download your JSON data from instagram (desktop version):

  1. Go to Settings → "See more in accounts centre" → "Your information and permissions"
  2. Click "Download or transfer information"
  3. Then select your account and then the types of information (you only need "Followers and following" but you can select other data if you want to look at it)
  4. Select "Download to device"
  5. Change the date range to "All time" and click save
  6. Change the format to "JSON" and click save
  7. Click "Create Files" and wait for your request to complete (should be fairly quick depending on how much info you requested)
  8. Click "Download" and re-enter your password

Then fetch the file containing your followers (probably called followers_1.json) and the one containing your following (probably called following.json), upload them here, and a list of people not following you back will appear :)
